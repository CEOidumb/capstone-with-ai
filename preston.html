About Me

.....

Growing up in Hackettstown, New Jersey, my passion for technology flourished

at The Hun School of Princeton, where I engaged in sports and a robust

coding program. Building my own computer further deepened my tech knowledge.

My journey progressed with the Wight Foundation introducing me to All Star

Code, an initiative empowering young men of color with essential tech skills,

networks, and mindsets. This program's blend of advanced coding, leadership,

and entrepreneurship aligns with my goals and athletic-honed resilience,

providing invaluable industry connections. I aim to leverage these experiences

to impact the tech world through innovative software, research, or leadership

in tech startups, driven by the desire to create positive change.
